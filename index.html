About

18 years old me at the 2018 GGJ, working on my first ever 2D game.

Hello travelers! I'm Tzach, a programmer from Tel Aviv, Israel. Gaming and game development have been my passions for years, and beyond work I'm drawn to exploring different cultures, spending time with family and pets, and catching up with friends.

My programming journey started in high school with C# and SQL, followed by three years as an Automation Developer at Mamram (IDF), scripting in Python, Bash, and Perl. During my service I discovered Unity and Israel's game dev community, which led to my first game dev role at Gavra Games.

I earned my BSc in Computer Science from Bar Ilan University, where I studied algorithms, OOP, and AI (NLP, image recognition). After graduating, I spent three years as a Software Developer at Check Point, building gateway applications in C, C++, and JavaScript.

Currently, I'm a Unity Developer at Communix, where I engineer scalable client-side architecture, optimize performance, and collaborate with cross-functional teams to ship gameplay features in Unity & C#. I'm thrilled to finally be living my dream of making games professionally!

Projects

Warriors : Rise to Glory

Unity C# 3D Physics Particle Systems

The result of my internship at Gavra Games. I created a dice mini-game where you and your opponent roll dice at your respective turns. The dice have values of Damage / Shield / Self Damage / Return, and based on luck, your goal is to either defeat your opponent, or have more HP than him.

The Descent

Unity C# Procedural Generation BFS Shaders

Final project for my CS degree. A rogue-like game where you descend into an Egyptian tomb, defeating mummies. The tomb is created at runtime with procedural generation, and enemies follow the player using BFS pathfinding. Features custom shaders and post-processing.

Governor

Unity C# Firebase Tech Design

Final project for my Advanced Unity Development course. Inspired by "Reigns", you manage a country as a prime minister based on advisors' recommendations. Built with proper tech design, class architecture, and Firebase API for card data and configuration.

Rob the Mob

Unity C# Microphone Input GGJ 2024

My 2024 GGJ game. Theme: "Make Me Laugh". Play a thief infiltrating the Illuminati and stealing objects from their members. When the boss makes a joke, you have to laugh (literally, the game uses a microphone!) or you will be suspected and caught!

LAB-yrinth

Unity C# 2D Puzzle GGJ 2018

My first 2D game (GGJ 2018). Theme: "Transmission". A doctor trapped by an evil genius must solve puzzles by moving color-coded robots to their targets using a transmission ray that changes color as it passes through different floors.
